## 🛰️ **PROJECT OVERVIEW**
Elite Cycling Lab (ECL) is a mobile-first biomechanical research project focused on bridging the gap between **raw kinematic data** and **on-road performance outcomes**.

Traditional bike fitting focuses on comfort and "neutral" ranges. ECL utilizes the **ECAM-87 (Elite Cycling Analysis Matrix)** to identify **Power Loss Vectors** and **Clinical Stability Leaks** during high-torque, real-world conditions.

---

## 🧠 **THE ECAM-87 ENGINE (PROPRIETARY LOGIC)**
ECAM-87 acts as a diagnostic software layer that sits on top of standard joint-angle capture. It processes raw data through a rules-based matrix to generate:

* **PCE (Power Conversion Efficiency):** A percentage-based score of mechanical advantage vs. power dissipation.
* **The Pelvic Stability Rule:** Detecting micro-oscillations >5° that correlate to core-to-crank torque leakage.
* **Cockpit–Respiratory Link:** Analyzing cervical and thoracic angles to optimize $VO_2$ max ceiling suppression in aerodynamic positions.
* **Clinical Handover Intelligence:** Automated identification of biomechanical markers consistent with specific injury profiles (e.g., PFPS, ITB Friction, Lower Back Instability).
